A second opinion helps you see further.

10/25/2025 · LinkedIn

When an engineer or manager asks for an independent review, it’s not because of uncertainty.

It’s to make sure the decision is truly optimal — not only technically, but also in terms of cost, risk, and future operation.

In complex projects, the right choice is not always obvious.
You can be an expert in technology and still not see the full picture — risks, timing, or long-term impact.
A second opinion helps you step outside your usual view and see how the solution looks from another angle.

It doesn’t replace responsibility — it strengthens it.
