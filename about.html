About PF Automation & Digital Solutions

We help industrial teams make confident automation and digitalization decisions with unbiased expertise and measurable results.

Who we are

PF ADS is an independent consulting partner focused on automation, control, and digitalization for industrial plants. We are vendor-neutral and funded only by our clients, so our advice is aligned with your objectives—not a supplier's sales target.

We bring multi-vendor, multi-platform experience across DCS, PLC, SCADA, MES, and APC, helping you de-risk modernization, procurement, and lifecycle decisions.

What we do

  • Independent audits and modernization roadmaps
  • Owner's Engineer support for tenders and vendor selection
  • Technical reviews of bids, migration plans, and TCO
  • Ongoing second-opinion advisory and project oversight

How we work

  • Objective: no reseller margins or referral fees
  • Transparent: clear scope, deliverables, and decision criteria
  • Pragmatic: actionable recommendations that fit your plant constraints
  • Data-driven: comparisons on risk, schedule, lifecycle cost, and operability

Why clients choose us

We help avoid costly missteps, negotiate stronger terms, and choose solutions that stand the test of time. Our clients value fast response, clear documentation, and decisions backed by evidence—not vendor promises.
